DIGITAL COMICS

Introduction

Digital comics are comic books or graphic novels that are available in digital format, making them accessible on various devices like smartphones, tablets, and computers. These comics often feature vibrant artwork and dynamic layouts, with some incorporating interactive elements such as animation or sound effects to enhance the reading experience. Digital platforms allow readers to easily browse, purchase, and store a vast collection of comics, making them a popular choice for fans who want convenient and on-the-go access to their favorite stories.
